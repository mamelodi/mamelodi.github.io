In this episode we partnered with Girls for Girls (G4G) teens to educate young girls on the usage
of sanitary pads, how to discard them, breaking down certain myths and
misconceptions held around the menstruation cycle, and the stages of the
menstruation cycle. Moreover, we tackled period poverty in our communities.
