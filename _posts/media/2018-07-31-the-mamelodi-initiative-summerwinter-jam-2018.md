The Mamelodi Initiative Jams are holiday programs that run 2-3 weeks. Summer Jam is in January and caters for 200 to 300 high school learners, and Winter Jam is during the June-July holiday and caters for 300 to 400 high school learners within Mamelodi and the surrounding communities.
