## Volunteer: From Mamelodi to University and Back

Meet Given Letsau, a Mamelodi youth who has now become a university student and
returned to volunteer as a teacher with the Mamelodi Initiative.

The Mamelodi Initiative runs on the dedication of volunteers like Given, proving
that lasting change comes from within the community itself.
