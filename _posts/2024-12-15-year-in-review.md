## Overview

2024 was another transformative year for The Mamelodi Initiative. We reached more students than ever before, expanded our programme offerings, and continued to see remarkable results in educational outcomes.

## Key Achievements

- **Summer Jam 2024**: 150 students participated in our intensive two-week programme
- **Winter Jam 2024**: 180 students attended our three-week holiday programme
- **Saturday Classes**: Weekly classes served 200+ students throughout the year
- **Computer Literacy**: 80 students gained essential digital skills
- **Youth Leadership Council**: 25 young leaders developed their leadership capacity

## Impact Metrics

Our students showed significant improvement across all programmes:

- 85% of Summer Jam participants reported increased confidence in their studies
- Average grade improvement of 12% for Saturday Classes attendees
- 95% of Winter Jam students completed the programme

## Looking Ahead

As we enter 2025, we're excited to expand our reach while deepening the impact of our existing programmes. Thank you to our donors, volunteers, and community partners who make this work possible.
