If you love kids and are passionate about teaching and making a difference, then the Mamelodi Initiative is looking for you!

## How to Apply

To volunteer with our programs, please contact our Program Director **Thato Mafokoane**:
- Email: [info@mamelodi.org](mailto:info@mamelodi.org)
- WhatsApp: +27 69 259 6435

Please include:
- Your background and relevant experience
- Which program(s) you're interested in
- Your availability
- Any questions you may have

We'll review your inquiry and get back to you with next steps!

---

## Looking for Career Opportunities?

We are not currently hiring for staff positions. However, if you're interested in longer-term involvement or career opportunities in the future, please reach out to Thato at the contact information above.
